Problem Prevention Guide

Pothole Prevention

Why Potholes Form:
  • Water seepage into road cracks
  • Freeze-thaw cycles weakening pavement
  • Heavy traffic load on weakened surfaces
Prevention Tips:
  • Report road cracks early
  • Keep storm drains clear
  • Support road maintenance budgets

Streetlight Maintenance

Importance:
  • Reduce crime and improve safety
  • Prevent traffic accidents
  • Enable safe pedestrian movement
What You Can Do:
  • Report burnt-out bulbs immediately
  • Keep fixtures free from debris
  • Trim tree branches blocking lights

Frequently Asked Questions

Emergency issues are addressed within 24 hours. Non-emergency issues like potholes typically take 3-7 business days, while larger infrastructure projects may take several weeks to complete.

Contact information is optional but recommended. It allows us to update you on progress and ask follow-up questions. You can also submit reports anonymously.

For life-threatening emergencies, always call 911 first. For utility emergencies, contact the appropriate utility company directly. You can report non-emergency issues through our platform for tracking.
